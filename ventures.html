Student Ventures:

Our ministry is dedicated to equipping students with the skills and principles necessary to navigate the workplace with integrity and respect for their faith. We believe that students can thrive professionally while staying true to their values. Through interactive workshops, mentorship programs, and networking opportunities, we empower students to embrace their faith in the workplace and become ambassadors of positive change. We emphasize the importance of ethical decision-making, fostering inclusive environments, and promoting respectful interactions with colleagues and superiors. Join us as we cultivate ageneration of future leaders who bring their faith into the workplace and make a lasting impact in their professional spheres.

Athletes In Action:
